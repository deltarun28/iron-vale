# Iron Vale

Iron Vale is a mobile-first, privacy-focused, single-player real-time territory control game.

The MVP is a small 1v1 strategy game played on a handcrafted hex map. Territories generate troops in real time. Players attack, reinforce, capture towns and capitals, use sea lanes, and manage a small gold economy.

The first version is intentionally simple. The goal is to prove the core game loop before adding extra systems.

## MVP Summary

Iron Vale MVP includes:

- 1v1 only
- 1 small handcrafted map
- Offline-first PWA target
- Canvas-rendered hex board
- HTML HUD
- Easy, Normal and Hard AI
- No multiplayer
- No accounts
- No ads
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No fog of war

## Core Game Loop

The player controls blue territories.

The AI controls red territories.

Neutral territories start unowned.

Each territory can generate troops over time. The player can send troops from one owned territory to an adjacent territory. If the target is owned by the player, troops reinforce it. If the target is neutral or enemy-owned, an attack is created.

Actions take time. Busy territories do not generate troops while they are busy.

The player wins by capturing all opponent-owned territories. Neutral territories do not need to be captured.

## Main Mechanics

### Troop Production

Territories generate troops over time based on terrain.

| Territory Type | Troop Generation |
|---|---:|
| Plains | +1 troop every 3 sec |
| Forest | +1 troop every 5 sec |
| Mountain | +1 troop every 7 sec |
| Capital | +1 troop every 3 sec |
| Neutral plains | +1 troop every 10 sec |
| Neutral forest | +1 troop every 15 sec |
| Neutral mountain | +1 troop every 20 sec |

### Production Caps

| Type | Normal Production Until | Slows Until | Stops At | Decays Above | Decays Toward |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Plains | 20 | 25 | 25 | 30 | 30 |
| Forest | 15 | 20 | 20 | 25 | 25 |
| Mountain | 10 | 15 | 15 | 25 | 25 |
| Capital | 20 | 25 | 25 | 35 | 35 |

### Gold

Gold is generated by towns and capitals only.

| Source | Gold Generation |
|---|---:|
| Capital | +1 gold every 4 sec |
| Town | +1 gold every 6 sec |
